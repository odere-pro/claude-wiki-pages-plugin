# presentation-independence

## Definition

The property of wiki pages whose meaning is separable from their rendered view. The Obsidian render is a view; the wiki page is the canonical form.

## Key Principles

- The property of wiki pages whose meaning is separable from their rendered view.
- Canonical term in the claude-wiki-pages **Ontology terms** vocabulary; conforms to the project glossary and is enforced by `validate-docs.sh`.
