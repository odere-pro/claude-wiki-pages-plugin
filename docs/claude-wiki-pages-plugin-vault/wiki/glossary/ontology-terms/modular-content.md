# modular content

## Definition

Content broken into typed, reusable page units (entities, concepts, topics) rather than monolithic documents, enabling flexible composition without duplication.

## Key Principles

- Content broken into typed, reusable page units (entities, concepts, topics) rather than monolithic documents, enabling flexible composition without duplication.
- Canonical term in the claude-wiki-pages **Ontology terms** vocabulary; conforms to the project glossary and is enforced by `validate-docs.sh`.
