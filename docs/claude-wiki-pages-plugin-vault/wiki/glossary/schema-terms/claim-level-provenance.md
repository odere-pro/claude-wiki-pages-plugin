# claim-level provenance

## Definition

The optional `source_quotes` field (schema v2): pins individual claims to the verbatim source sentence behind them, complementing page-level `sources`.

## Key Principles

- The optional `source_quotes` field (schema v2): pins individual claims to the verbatim source sentence behind them, complementing page-level `sources`.
- Canonical term in the claude-wiki-pages **Schema terms** vocabulary; conforms to the project glossary and is enforced by `validate-docs.sh`.

- `sources`
